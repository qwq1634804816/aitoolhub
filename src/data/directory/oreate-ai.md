Oreate AI is a specialized academic writing aid tool targeting students, scholars, and researchers. Leveraging its specially trained AI models, users can easily complete various types of academic papers. The platform integrates three core features: content generation, paraphrasing, and citation, supporting the generation of articles up to 20,000 words and the rewriting and polishing of existing manuscripts up to 10,000 words.

Moreover, Oreate AI includes a built-in research assistant that allows users to search for and cite academic resources directly within the platform, supporting multiple mainstream citation formats such as APA, MLA, and Chicago, thus ensuring citation accuracy and compliance. Equipped with plagiarism detection and intelligent editing features, the platform helps improve the originality and quality of papers, aiming to simplify the writing process and enhance the efficiency and standardization of academic work.

## User Guide

Oreate AI is a web-based online platform requiring no installation; users can directly access the official website to use it. It aims to provide a one-stop service for academic writing, fully supporting the entire process from idea conception and information retrieval to writing and plagiarism checking.

### 1. Registration and Login

1. Open a browser and navigate to the Oreate AI official website: `https://www.oreateai.com/home/index`.
2. New users click the “Try for free” or “Start Writing - for free” button and follow the process to complete registration.
3. Registered users click “Log in” and enter their account details to log in.

### 2. Core Function Operation Procedures

#### 1. Start a New Article
After logging in, enter the “All-in-one editor” where you can write directly or use AI generation to create a draft.

#### 2. AI Paper Generation (Long-form Writing)
- Select the paper generation option in the editor.
- Input detailed writing topics or instructions; clear instructions help improve generation quality, for example, “Analyze the impact of social media on adolescent consumer behavior.”
- Oreate AI generates content with clear structure and coherent logic based on the instructions.
- Long texts can be generated chapter by chapter, supporting up to 20,000 words.

#### 3. Paraphrasing and Polishing (Paraphrase & Humanize)
- Paste text not exceeding 10,000 words into the editor.
- Highlight the text and enable the “Paraphrase” function to produce a version with changed structure and wording but the same meaning.
- If the text appears too artificial, use the “Humanize” function to make the expression more natural and fluent.

#### 4. Research and Add Citations (Research & Citation)
- Use the “Research Tool” in the writing interface to enter keywords and search for related academic information.
- Insert directly into the text and choose citation formats such as APA, MLA, or Chicago.
- The system automatically generates a formatted reference list at the end, reducing the difficulty of manual compilation.

#### 5. Intelligent Editing and Plagiarism Detection
- Intelligent editing can shorten, expand, or academicize selected paragraphs upon request.
- Plagiarism detection scans the entire document, producing a similarity report to aid revision and ensure originality.

#### 6. Formatting and Exporting
- Automatically formats the article and supports downloads in multiple formats including Word and PDF.

Through these steps, users can efficiently complete drafting, rewriting, plagiarism checking, and final exporting of papers, fully covering the academic writing process.
