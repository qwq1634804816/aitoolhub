Kimi Chat is an intelligent assistant created by Moonshot AI, capable of handling texts up to 200,000 Chinese characters in length. With its enormous memory capacity, it can quickly browse documents and perform online information retrieval, bringing users efficient information processing and interactive experience. It breaks through the limitations of similar products in long-text processing, supports various API calls, and is easy to integrate into different application scenarios. Its core technology has already been adopted by multiple heavyweight products in the industry.

## Kimi Chat Feature List

- Intelligent Conversation: Kimi can engage with users in multi-turn, multi-topic, and multi-style dialogues, supporting both Chinese and English, covering fields such as entertainment, education, technology, business, health, and more.
- Information Query: Kimi can help users quickly obtain various types of information from the internet, such as encyclopedias, news, hot events, weather, maps, stocks, and exchange rates, providing concise and accurate answers.
- Professional Advice: Based on users’ questions and needs, Kimi can offer reasonable and professional suggestions covering tourism, diet, shopping, learning, work, and daily life.
- Content Generation: Kimi can generate various creative contents based on user input, such as poetry, stories, code, songs, celebrity impersonations, etc., and supports editing and optimizing the content.
