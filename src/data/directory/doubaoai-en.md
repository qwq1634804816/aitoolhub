## Comprehensive Introduction to Doubao

Doubao is an AI assistant developed by a TikTok subsidiary, utilizing the latest domestic Lark large model. It aims to help users solve problems, access information, and improve work efficiency. Doubao supports communication in both Chinese and English, can be used online, and provides web, Android, and iOS versions. Users can register by logging in with their phone number, TikTok account, or Apple ID. Doubao comes with a rich set of AI agents suitable for diverse scenarios such as knowledge Q&A, copywriting, and image generation. It can also interact with users in a natural and friendly manner to inspire creativity, assist with creation, and provide emotional companionship. Doubao is your trustworthy AI partner—welcome to experience and explore!

> Doubao is based on ByteDance’s proprietary large language model, rumored to include distilled data from GPT-4.
>
> The overseas versions of Doubao are CICI (https://www.ciciai.com/) and another similar product, ChitChop, with comparable functions.
>
> If conditions permit, it is recommended to use the COZE and CICI combination for deeper application scenarios and to enjoy a higher-quality free experience with the GPT-4 model.
>
> According to Forbes, TikTok’s parent company ByteDance has quietly launched four generative AI applications in the past three months, including Cici AI, Coze, ChitChop, and BagelBell (https://www.kdjingpai.com/bagelbellaiwenzi/), accumulating millions of downloads. The new apps and their terms of service do not explicitly mention ByteDance; three of them are operated by Spring (SG) Pte., while one is managed by ByteDance’s subsidiary Poligon Pte., known for producing adult web novels and games for ByteDance. According to Google Play data, Cici is the highest downloaded among them, exceeding ten million times.

## Doubao Feature List

- AI Chat: Covers a variety of topics, providing objective and detailed replies, suitable for passing time and exploring hard-to-search knowledge.
- Writing Assistant: Offers valuable reference suggestions, with content quality occasionally being somewhat uniform.
- Image Generation: Creates images based on user descriptions or examples, generally yielding good results, though occasional abnormal images may appear.
- Music Radio: Plays high-quality music according to user preferences or scenarios, with a rich music library.
- Baby Cutie (Naiwa Xiaomengmeng): Interacts adorably with users, expressive, has a sweet voice, capable of singing and dancing.
- AI Comic Generation: Draws multi-style comics based on descriptions or examples, with visually appealing scenes though occasionally inconsistent plot logic.

## Doubao Usage Help

- On the web and Android clients, users can directly input text or voice to communicate with Doubao.
- The iOS version requires installation via TestFlight, afterward interaction can be done via text or voice.
- Users can select or customize their preferred AI agents in settings, adjusting speaking style, skills, and background.
- The voice input/output mode, voice tone, and speed can be adjusted through settings.
- Theme colors can be changed, and font size and style can be customized.
- Users can view and clear chat history.
