Voyagard is an AI writing assistance platform specially designed for students, researchers, and academic professionals. Unlike general AI chatbots, Voyagard centers on the core design concepts of "academic compliance" and "full-process support," not only assisting in content generation but also embedding features such as literature management, polishing, citation formatting, and plagiarism checking, thereby constructing a comprehensive academic writing environment.

In rigorous academic writing, the requirements for formatting and originality are very strict. Voyagard supports the entire process from inspiration recording, draft writing to finalizing within a unified interface. It incorporates language models optimized for academic scenarios, capable of providing wording suggestions that comply with paper standards. At the same time, its plagiarism detection refers to Turnitin’s logic to help users identify citation risks in advance. It automatically handles the cumbersome APA and MLA citation formats, allowing users to focus solely on building their arguments without worrying about formatting details.

## Features List

- **Intelligent Academic Polishing**: Offers language optimization suggestions targeted at academic papers, including sentence structure adjustment, vocabulary upgrading, and tone refinement, enhancing the professional level of the article.
- **Automatic Citation Management**: Supports mainstream citation formats such as APA, MLA, Chicago, etc. After users input bibliographic information or links, the system automatically generates standardized in-text citations and bibliography.
- **AI Plagiarism and Compliance Detection**: Built-in plagiarism detection highlights text segments similar to existing literature and provides rewriting suggestions to help reduce duplication rate and maintain academic integrity.
- **Literature Dialogue Assistant**: Supports uploading PDF papers or notes. Through AI dialogues, it quickly extracts core viewpoints, abstracts, and data, assisting in constructing literature reviews.
- **Long Document Writing Mode**: An editor designed specifically for long-form papers, providing outline views and chapter management to avoid lag and logical confusion when handling long documents.
- **Multilingual Support**: Although primarily optimized for English academic writing, it also supports non-native English users in accurately converting their ideas into authentic academic English.

## Getting Started

As a web-based online service, Voyagard eliminates the need for complicated client installation. Users can start writing directly through a browser. Below is a quick start guide.

### 1. Account Registration and Workspace Setup

After visiting the official website, register an account via the “Get Started” or “Sign Up” button at the top right corner, using Google login or email registration. After logging in, enter the personal dashboard and click “New Project” to create a new project. Choose the writing type (e.g., Essay, Research Paper, Thesis, etc.) and set the target citation format (e.g., APA 7th Edition); the system will automatically configure document standards.

### 2. Import Literature or Write Content

The editor interface has a navigation bar on the left, an auxiliary toolbar on the right, and the main writing area in the center.

- **Import Literature**: Click the “Library” or “Sources” icon on the right to upload PDFs or paste literature links. The system automatically parses metadata (title, author, year).
- **Draft Writing**: Enter the title and outline in the main writing area. If encountering writer’s block, input prompt words, press <code>Ctrl+J</code> or click the AI generation button, and the AI will help continue ideas or expand arguments.
- **Literature Dialogue**: Select an uploaded PDF, input relevant questions (such as summarizing research methods) in the dialog box on the right. The AI quickly extracts key points that can be directly inserted into the main text as citation support.

### 3. Citation and Polishing

Core function area introduction:

- **Insert Citation**: When citing a viewpoint, place the cursor at the end of the sentence, click "Cite" on the toolbar, select literature. The system automatically inserts a format-compliant citation (e.g., <code>(Smith, 2023)</code>) and synchronizes the reference list at the end.
- **Academic Polishing**: Highlight text and click the floating menu options "Improve" or "Academic Polish." The system offers various modification suggestions, eliminating colloquialisms and enhancing academic coherence.

### 4. Plagiarism Check and Export

After completing the article, click the “Plagiarism Check” button at the top to run the check. The system scans the entire text and generates a report. Highlighted parts can be optimized and rewritten with the built-in "Paraphrase" tool to reduce similarity.

Once confirmed error-free, export the document via the “Export” button at the top right, supporting Word (.docx), PDF, and LaTeX formats. Exported files retain formatting and citation links for direct submission.

## Application Scenarios

1. **International Students Completing Final Essays**

Many non-native English speaking students lose points due to limited vocabulary and simple sentence patterns. Voyagard allows them to write a simple draft first and then use the polishing feature to elevate the article to university academic standards while automatically handling complex MLA formatting, preventing point deductions from formatting errors.

2. **Researchers Writing Literature Reviews**

When preparing proposals or journal articles, researchers need to read many papers. The literature dialogue function quickly extracts core conclusions from dozens of papers, and combined with citation management, efficiently organizes them into a logically rigorous review without switching between Zotero and Word.

3. **Academic Editors Proofreading Manuscripts**

Supervisors or editors can use Voyagard as a preliminary screening tool to quickly identify potential plagiarism risks and leverage AI to correct grammatical errors, allowing more time to focus on article logic and innovation.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. **Is Voyagard free?**

Voyagard adopts a freemium model. Free users enjoy basic writing and limited AI generation times. The professional version offers unlimited plagiarism checks, advanced polishing models, and unlimited literature storage. Please refer to the official website’s Pricing page for details.

2. **Will using Voyagard be considered AI writing?**

Voyagard aims to assist rather than fully replace writing. Fully relying on AI-generated full texts may be detected as AI writing. However, its "Humanize" and rewriting tools encourage users to create secondary works based on AI suggestions. Reasonable use of polishing and citation functions generally conforms to academic norms and is safe.

3. **Does it support academic writing in Chinese?**

Voyagard is mainly trained on English academic literature and performs best in English writing. Although it supports Chinese commands and basic polishing, support for Chinese-specific citation standards (e.g., China National Standard GB/T 7714) is limited. It is recommended to be used primarily for English writing.
