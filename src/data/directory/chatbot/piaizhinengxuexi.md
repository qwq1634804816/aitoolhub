Inflection AI is an innovative company focused on building AI products for consumers. Its core product, Pi, is a personal intelligent chat assistant. Pi leverages cutting-edge generative AI technology to engage users in natural and fluent conversations, providing support, advice, answers, and creative content. Users can learn new skills, create various plans, regulate emotions, and explore hobbies with Pi’s help. Pi is designed to be a warm and thoughtful companion that always responds to users’ needs and personalities.

![Pi.AI Smart Learning Assistant](https://repo.alisencent.com/upload/file/2026/04/01/8f96d1a3de588e9.png)

## Features

- Conversation: Pi understands complex natural language input and provides personalized responses
- Advice: Gives practical suggestions and guidance based on user status and goals
- Answers: Responds to all kinds of questions, whether important or trivial, to satisfy user curiosity
- Creativity: Generates stories, poems, lyrics, and other text content to inspire imagination
- Learning: Supports users mastering new skills like programming, foreign languages, and music, offering tutorials and exercises
- Planning: Assists users in planning careers, health, travel, etc., and provides execution tools
- Emotions: Helps manage stress, anxiety, and other emotions, offering listening and comfort
- Interests: Helps explore interests such as movies, music, and games, providing recommendations and reviews

## User Guide

1. Download the Pi app or visit the official website to register and log in
2. Introduce yourself to Pi so it can learn your basic information and interests
3. Choose communication mode (text or voice) and the platform to use
4. Start chatting with Pi, asking questions, sharing ideas, or selecting topics anytime
5. Provide feedback, telling Pi which responses you like or dislike to help it learn your preferences
6. Access settings to adjust Pi’s appearance, voice, personality, and user preferences
7. Review the data privacy policy to understand how data is collected, used, and managed
