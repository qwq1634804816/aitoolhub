Tiangong AI is China’s first intelligent assistant that integrates multiple functions including search, dialogue, creation, document parsing, painting, and PPT production. Utilizing cutting-edge AI technology, it accurately grasps user intentions, retrieves, and consolidates vast information from the internet, presenting high-quality ad-free search results. Meanwhile, Tiangong AI also supports interactive chat, content creation, Q&A, and image generation to meet diverse user needs in academics, work, and daily life.

## Feature List

- **AI Search**: Accurately understands user needs, providing ad-free and high-quality search results.
- **AI Conversation**: Interacts with users through natural language processing, fulfilling diverse demands such as Q&A and logical reasoning.
- **AI Writing**: Assists in generating various types of copywriting, enriching writing styles.
- **AI Document Analysis**: Quickly and deeply analyzes multi-format documents, supporting academic and research-oriented information retrieval.
- **AI Painting**: Creates images based on user descriptions, supporting various painting styles.
- **AI PPT**: Efficiently generates professional and visually appealing PPT presentations.
- **AI Music**: Helps users realize their dreams of music creation.
- **Code Programming**: Supports code writing and debugging.


With a 200-billion-parameter large language model comparable to ChatGPT, it can achieve:

- Natural language interaction
- Manuscript generation and knowledge Q&A
- Logical reasoning and mathematical calculations
- Programming assistance and code generation
- Support for long text dialogues and multi-turn conversations


## Usage Help

### Installation and Login

1. Visit the official Tiangong AI website: [Tiangong AI](https://www.tiangong.cn/)
2. Click “Login” at the top right of the page, register an account using phone number or email.
3. After registration, download and install the client supporting Windows, Mac, and mobile devices.

### Feature Operation Guide

#### AI Search

1. Enter keywords in the search box, click “Search.”
2. Tiangong AI will automatically understand the search intent and return high-quality, ad-free information.
3. Users can organize the results into mind maps or outlines for convenient future use.

#### AI Conversation

1. Enter the “Conversation” module, input questions or requests.
2. Tiangong AI provides detailed answers and suggestions through natural language interaction.
3. Supports knowledge Q&A, logical reasoning, mathematical calculations, and more.

#### AI Writing

1. Open the “Writing” module, select the writing style (article, report, etc.).
2. Input the topic and requirements, Tiangong AI automatically generates a draft.
3. Users can edit and refine the content, finally producing a satisfactory manuscript.

#### AI Document Analysis

1. Upload the document to be analyzed (supports formats like PDF, Word, etc.).
2. Tiangong AI quickly parses the document content and generates detailed reports.
3. Assists professional academic research and information retrieval.

#### AI Painting

1. Enter the “Painting” module, describe the imagined image.
2. Tiangong AI generates works based on the description, supporting adjustments and optimization.
3. Various painting styles to satisfy diverse creative needs.

#### AI PPT

1. Select the “PPT” module, input the topic and requirements.
2. Tiangong AI automatically creates professional and visually appealing PPTs, supporting editing and beautification.
3. Multiple templates and styles help quickly complete presentations.

#### AI Music

1. Enter the “Music” module, choose the music style.
2. Tiangong AI composes music based on the user’s needs, supporting adjustments and optimization.
3. Realizes users’ dreams of music creation.

#### Code Programming

1. Enter the “Code” module, select programming language and project type.
2. Input coding requirements, Tiangong AI automatically generates and debugs code.
3. Users can modify and perfect to complete project development.
