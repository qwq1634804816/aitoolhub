Conch Wenwen AI is a conversational intelligent assistant built using advanced AI generation technology. Through a concise chat interface, it can understand user needs and provide corresponding services. Besides helping to answer questions and acquire knowledge, it can also inspire writing creativity and aid in quick information retrieval. This tool is widely applicable in scenarios such as academic research, writing creation, and daily life assistance.

Conch Wenwen AI integrates [MiniMax](https://www.minimaxi.com/) multimodal capabilities, able to accurately recognize image content, especially with high accuracy in recognizing English and mathematical formula characters, though the recognition of Chinese characters is slightly less effective.


![Conch Wenwen AI Official Homepage](https://repo.alisencent.com/upload/file/2026/03/31/190a1ec74129a19.png)

## Conch Wenwen AI Feature List

- **Search Q&A**: Conch Wenwen can accurately grasp your intentions and provide practical suggestions and content support. Whether for life advice, copywriting, resume design, or meeting minutes creation, it can generate high-quality content. Additionally, it can combine online information to help you search for relevant materials and assist in decision-making.

- **Creative Writing**: Whether it is social media content, academic papers, or marketing copy, simply enter keywords or a beginning, and Conch Wenwen will generate a complete text, also supporting adjustments and optimization based on feedback. Moreover, it can produce creative works such as poems, stories, and lyrics, stimulating writing inspiration.

- **Voice Interaction**: Provides natural and smooth voice model support, enabling voice-based question answering and real-time voice conversations without obvious foreign accents. Users can also train their exclusive voice model easily by reading specified texts.

- **Code Writing**: Supports code generation in various programming languages (such as Python, Java, C++). After users input requirements or comments, Conch Wenwen can automatically generate code, with additional functions of error detection and performance optimization.

- **Language Translation**: Supports translation services in multiple languages including English, Chinese, Japanese, French, and more. After inputting or voicing content to be translated, Conch Wenwen can provide accurate and contextually appropriate translation results.

### Conch Wenwen AI Document Assistant

Supports uploading files within 100MB in TXT, DOCX, PDF, PNG, JPEG, JPG formats. Usage includes:

- Upload private materials for Conch Wenwen to answer questions.
- Have Conch Wenwen translate entire documents (not sentence-by-sentence translation).
- Quickly extract core document content or ask questions about contents.
- Upload images to assist Conch Wenwen in understanding and describing image information.
